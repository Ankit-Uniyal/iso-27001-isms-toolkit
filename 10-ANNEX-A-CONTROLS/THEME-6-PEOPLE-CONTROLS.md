## Key HR and IS Coordination Points

### Pre-Employment (6.1)
HR manages the screening process. IS team defines the screening requirements for roles with access to Highly Restricted data or regulated systems. Screening results reviewed by HR Director; CISO notified for any adverse findings in IS-sensitive roles.

### Joiners Process (6.2, 6.3)
IS team delivers IS awareness induction within 5 working days of start date. IT Service Desk provisions access per approved role profile. HR ensures IS obligations included in employment contract before start date.

### Movers Process (6.5)
Line Manager notifies IT within 5 business days of role change. IT reviews and adjusts access per new role. ISMS Manager informed of significant role changes affecting ISMS responsibilities.

### Leavers Process (6.5)
HR notifies IT on date leaver is confirmed. All access revoked on last working day (or earlier for risk reasons). Equipment returned and wiped. Exit interview includes reminder of post-employment IS obligations. IT confirms access revocation to ISMS Manager.

### No-Blame Reporting Culture (6.8)
NFS actively promotes a no-blame, just culture for IS event reporting. Staff are not disciplined for reporting suspected incidents in good faith. The CISO communicates this at each annual awareness training. This is critical for early incident detection.
