## Purpose

This Risk Treatment Plan records the treatment decisions for all information security risks identified in the Risk Register (ISMS-RISKRG-001). It documents the selected treatment option, the Annex A controls applied, implementation actions, owners, and target dates.

The plan must be approved by risk owners before implementation begins.

## Treatment Option Definitions

| Option | Description | When Used |
|---|---|---|
| **Treat (Modify)** | Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact | Most IS risks |
| **Accept** | Formally accept the risk — document and monitor | LOW risks within appetite; residual risks post-treatment |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to third party (cyber insurance, outsourcing) | Where another party manages better or insurable |
| **Avoid** | Cease the activity causing the risk | HIGH/CRITICAL risks that cannot be reduced to appetite |
| **Share** | Distribute the risk across multiple parties | Shared infrastructure; joint ventures |
