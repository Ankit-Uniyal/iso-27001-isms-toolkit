## Finding Classification

| Category | Definition | Response |
|---|---|---|
| **Major NC** | Significant failure; puts ISMS outcomes at risk | Corrective action plan within 20 working days |
| **Minor NC** | Single lapse; not systemic | Corrective action within 30 working days |
| **OFI** | Opportunity for improvement; not a NC | No mandatory response |
| **Positive** | Good practice | Recognise and share |
