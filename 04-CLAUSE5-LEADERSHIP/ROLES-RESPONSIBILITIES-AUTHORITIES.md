## Key Role Descriptions

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO) / Top Management
- Demonstrate leadership and commitment to the ISMS (Clause 5.1)
- Ensure IS Policy and objectives are compatible with strategic direction
- Ensure integration of ISMS into business processes
- Ensure resources are available
- Review and approve the IS Policy

### Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
- Overall accountability for ISMS design, implementation, and effectiveness
- Report to top management on ISMS performance
- Chair the IS Steering Committee
- Own the IS risk management process
- Approve the Statement of Applicability
- Ensure ISMS conforms to ISO 27001:2022 requirements

### ISMS Manager / IS Lead
- Day-to-day management of ISMS documentation and processes
- Maintain ISMS document master list
- Coordinate internal audits and management reviews
- Track corrective actions and improvement activities
- Liaise with certification body

### IT Security Team
- Implement and operate technical IS controls
- Monitor security events and operate SIEM
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and manage patching
- Investigate IS incidents
- Provide technical input to risk assessments

### IT Operations
- Implement infrastructure security controls
- Manage system access and authentication
- Maintain backup and recovery systems
- Support physical and environmental security

### Human Resources (HR)
- Conduct pre-employment screening (Control 6.1)
- Include IS obligations in employment contracts (Control 6.2)
- Deliver IS induction for new starters
- Manage leavers process — access revocation (Control 6.5)
- Support disciplinary process for IS breaches (Control 6.4)

### Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- Ensure GDPR / DPA 2018 compliance
- Maintain Article 30 records of processing activities
- Conduct DPIAs for high-risk processing
- Handle data subject access requests
- Notify ICO of reportable data breaches within 72 hours

### Legal / Compliance
- Maintain legal and regulatory requirements register
- Provide legal advice on IS obligations
- Draft and review IS contractual clauses
- Manage regulatory relationships

### All Staff
- Complete mandatory IS awareness training
- Follow IS policies, procedures, and acceptable use policy
- Report suspected IS incidents immediately
- Protect information assets in their custody
- Follow clear desk / clear screen policy
