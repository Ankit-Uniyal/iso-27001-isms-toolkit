# ISMS Communications Plan
## ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Clause 7.4

**Organisation:** Nexus Financial Services Ltd (NFS)
**Document Reference:** NFS-IS-PLAN-002
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 01 May 2026
**Review Date:** 01 May 2027
**Owner:** ISMS Manager
**Approved By:** Chief Information Security Officer

---

## 1. Purpose

This Communications Plan defines what information security communications will take place at NFS, when they will occur, who is responsible, and who the audience is. It supports Clause 7.4 of ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (Communication).

---

## 2. Communications Matrix

### 2.1 Internal Communications

| # | Communication | What | When | Who Communicates | To Whom | Channel | Record Kept |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | ISMS Management Review Output | Results of management review, decisions, action items | Following each management review (minimum annual) | CISO | Board, SLT, ISMS Team | Minutes circulated via email | Yes — meeting minutes |
| 2 | IS Policy Updates | New or revised IS policies | Upon policy change; minimum annual | CISO | All staff | Intranet, email notification | Yes — acknowledgement log |
| 3 | IS Awareness Newsletter | Security tips, threat alerts, reminders | Monthly | ISMS Manager | All staff | Email newsletter | Yes — distribution list |
| 4 | Phishing Simulation Results | Aggregate results and lessons learned | Following each simulation (quarterly) | ISMS Manager | All staff, Line Managers | Email + intranet | Yes — report |
| 5 | Security Incident Notification | Confirmed security incidents | Within 24 hours of confirmation | CISO | Affected departments, SLT | Email + call | Yes — incident log |
| 6 | Risk Register Updates | Changes to risk register | Quarterly or upon material change | ISMS Manager | Risk Committee, CISO | Risk Committee meeting | Yes — risk register |
| 7 | IS KPI Dashboard | Monthly IS metrics and performance | Monthly | ISMS Manager | CISO, Board Risk Committee | Dashboard report | Yes — KPI log |
| 8 | Audit Findings | Internal audit results and actions | Following each audit | Lead Internal Auditor | Auditee, CISO, SLT | Audit report | Yes — audit register |
| 9 | IS Objectives Progress | Progress against annual IS objectives | Quarterly | ISMS Manager | CISO, Board | Board paper | Yes — objectives tracker |
| 10 | Staff IS Briefings | ISMS updates, new threats, policy reminders | Quarterly | ISMS Manager | All staff via managers | Team briefings | No |

### 2.2 External Communications

| # | Communication | What | When | Who Communicates | To Whom | Channel | Record Kept |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Regulatory Reporting — FCA | IS incidents reportable to FCA under SYSC | Within regulatory timeframes | CISO + Legal | Financial Conduct Authority | FCA Connect portal | Yes — regulatory log |
| 2 | ICO Data Breach Notification | Personal data breaches | Within 72 hours of awareness (UK GDPR Art 33) | DPO + CISO | Information Commissioner's Office | ICO online portal | Yes — breach register |
| 3 | Customer Data Breach Notification | Notification to affected data subjects | Without undue delay where risk is high | DPO | Affected customers | Letter, email, or online | Yes — notification log |
| 4 | ISMS Certification Body | Audit scheduling, corrective actions, ISMS updates | As required; annual surveillance | ISMS Manager | Certification body (BSI/LRQA) | Email, audit platform | Yes — certification file |
| 5 | Supplier Security Requirements | NFS IS requirements for new/existing suppliers | During onboarding; annually for key suppliers | Procurement + ISMS Manager | Suppliers and third parties | Contractual; supplier questionnaire | Yes — supplier register |
| 6 | Law Enforcement / Legal | Evidence preservation, lawful disclosure | Upon receipt of valid legal order | CISO + Legal | Police, courts, legal authorities | Secure channel | Yes — legal log |
| 7 | Insurance Notifications | Cyber insurance incident notification | Per policy terms (typically within 72 hours) | CISO + Finance | Cyber insurer | Email + insurer portal | Yes — insurance file |

## 3. Escalation Communication Matrix

### 3.1 Security Incident Communication Escalation

| Severity | Timeframe | Who Is Notified | Communication Method |
|---|---|---|---|
| P1 — Critical | Immediately (within 1 hour) | CISO, CEO, CTO, Legal, PR | Phone call + email |
| P2 — High | Within 4 hours | CISO, affected business owners | Email + Slack |
| P3 — Medium | Within 24 hours | ISMS Manager, IT Manager | Email |
| P4 — Low | Within 5 business days | ISMS Manager | Email or ticketing system |

### 3.2 Regulatory Notification Timelines

| Regulator | Trigger | Notification Deadline | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| ICO | Personal data breach with risk to individuals | 72 hours from awareness | DPO |
| FCA | Material operational incident | Per FCA guidance (SYSC 8.4) | CISO + Legal |
| PCI DSS / Acquirer | Payment card data compromise | Immediately upon discovery | CISO + Finance |

## 5. Communication Records

All formal IS communications are retained as documented evidence in accordance with the Document Control Procedure (NFS-IS-PRO-007). Minimum retention periods:

- Management review communications: 3 years
- Incident notifications (internal and external): 5 years
- Regulatory correspondence: 7 years
- Staff awareness records: 3 years
