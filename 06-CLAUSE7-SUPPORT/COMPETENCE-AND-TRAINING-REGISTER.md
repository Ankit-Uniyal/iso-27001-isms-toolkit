# Competence and Training Register

**Organisation:** Nexus Financial Services Ltd (NFS)
**Document Reference:** NFS-IS-REG-003

**Effective Date:** 01 May 2026

**Owner:** ISMS Manager
**Approved By:** Chief Information Security Officer

---

## 1. Purpose

This register documents the information security competence requirements for roles with ISMS responsibilities, records training completed, and tracks compliance with Clause 7.2 (Competence) and 7.3 (Awareness) of ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

---

## 2. Competence Requirements by Role

### 2.1 ISMS Core Team

| Role | Required Competence | Minimum Qualification / Experience |
|---|---|---|
| CISO | Strategic IS management, risk management, regulatory compliance, incident response | ISO 27001 Lead Auditor or Lead Implementer; 5+ years IS management experience |
| ISMS Manager | ISMS implementation, internal audit, document control, risk assessment | ISO 27001 Lead Implementer or equivalent; 3+ years IS experience |
| IS Analyst | Vulnerability management, security monitoring, incident investigation | CompTIA Security+, CISSP or equivalent; 2+ years IS experience |
| DPO (Data Protection Officer) | UK GDPR, data subject rights, privacy by design | CIPP/E, BCS DPPA or equivalent; 2+ years DP experience |
| IT Security Engineer | Network security, endpoint protection, access control, cryptography | CCNA Security, CEH or equivalent; 3+ years IT security experience |

### 2.2 Business-as-Usual Roles

| Role Category | Required IS Competence |
|---|---|
| All Staff | IS awareness, AUP compliance, incident reporting, phishing recognition |
| Line Managers | IS responsibilities of their team, escalation paths, access request management |
| IT Staff | Secure configuration, patch management, access provisioning, incident handling |
| Finance / Payroll | Financial data handling, fraud awareness, PCI DSS basics |
| HR | Pre-employment screening, leavers process, disciplinary procedures for IS |
| Third-Party / Contractors | NFS IS policies, data handling requirements, incident reporting |

## 7. Training Record Retention

Training completion records are retained for a minimum of 3 years in the NFS Learning Management System (LMS). Records include: staff name, role, training module, date completed, score achieved, and certificate (where applicable).

---

## 8. Register Maintenance

This register is updated quarterly by the ISMS Manager and reviewed during annual management reviews. Non-compliance with mandatory training triggers escalation to the relevant line manager and HR.
