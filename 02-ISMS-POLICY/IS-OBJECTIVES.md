# Information Security Objectives

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022 | Clause 6.2

**Document ID:** ISMS-OBJ-001
**Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** CISO | **Date:** ___ | **Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## Purpose

This document establishes measurable Information Security Objectives in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 6.2. Objectives are aligned with the Information Security Policy and reviewed at each management review.

---

## IS Objectives Table

| # | Objective | Measure / KPI | Target | Measurement Frequency | Data Source | Owner | Current Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| OBJ-01 | Reduce the number of confirmed IS incidents | Number of confirmed IS incidents per quarter | <= 3 per quarter | Quarterly | IS incident register | CISO | ___ |
| OBJ-02 | Ensure all staff complete annual IS awareness training | % staff completing annual IS training | >= 95% by 31 March each year | Annual | LMS training records | HR / CISO | ___ |
| OBJ-03 | Maintain patch compliance on all critical systems | % critical systems with patches applied within 30 days of release | >= 98% | Monthly | Vulnerability management tool | IT Security | ___ |
| OBJ-04 | Achieve and maintain ISO 27001:2022 certification | Certification status | Certified and no major NCs at surveillance audit | Annual | Certification body audit report | CISO | ___ |
| OBJ-05 | Ensure IS risk assessments completed on schedule | % risk assessments completed on time | 100% | Quarterly | Risk register | ISMS Manager | ___ |
| OBJ-06 | Complete all internal audit actions within agreed timeframes | % audit actions closed on time | >= 90% | Quarterly | Audit action tracker | ISMS Manager | ___ |
| OBJ-07 | Maintain low mean time to detect (MTTD) IS incidents | MTTD for IS incidents | <= 24 hours | Quarterly | SIEM / incident log | IT Security | ___ |
| OBJ-08 | Reduce supplier IS risk | % of critical suppliers with completed IS assessments | 100% of critical; >= 80% of high | Annual | Supplier risk register | Procurement / IS | ___ |
| OBJ-09 | Protect customer and employee personal data | Number of personal data breaches reportable to ICO | 0 per year | Annual | Incident register / DPO | DPO | ___ |
| OBJ-10 | Maintain IS business continuity | Recovery Time Objective (RTO) for critical systems met in BC test | 100% | Annual (BC test) | BC test report | IT Operations | ___ |

## Objective Planning Requirements (per Clause 6.2)

For each IS Objective, the following must be determined:

| Field | Requirement |
|---|---|
| What will be done | Specific actions to achieve the objective |
| Resources required | Budget, tools, personnel |
| Who will be responsible | Named owner |
| When completed | Target date |
| How results will be evaluated | Measurement method and reporting |
